Protect your vehicle by various recording modes

Parking Recording

Normal Recording (Continuous Recording while driving)

The DAS-7000FHD records continuously after powering up in crystal clear, “High Definition” video using 2.2M Pixels Color CMOS Full HD camera for front and 2M Pixels Color CMOS HD camera for rear side.
Vehicle location, speed, direction of travel, date and time are recorded through the optional GPS module and saved to Micro SD card for later retrieval.

The DAS-7000FHD can also be configured to record while parked, recording vehicle interference or car accidents while you are absent. The high definition ultra clear images can be used to identify vehicle number plates and can be used as evidence in legal proceedings and insurance claims to determine accident blame or liability.

Event Recording (detected upon impact while driving and parking)

In addition to continuous recording, the DAS-7000FHD automatically records special “Event” videos such as car accidents, into a separate “EVT” memory folder. Each event video file is triggered through the detection of shock jolting, sudden braking or acceleration, and sharp swerving of the vehicle.

Manual Recording

You can easily control recording by one simple clicking when you driving or stay on the load with vehicle ignition is on. There are two buttons on the back of DAS-7000FHD’s main device and if you press above button shortly, recording file at that moment is saved as ‘Manual Recording’ file with shutter sound of voice guidance, into ‘MAN’ folder inside of SD memory card and you can easy to find out it.

If you have some dangerous moment in emergency situation, you can use this Manual Recording function to save evidence as video recorded file.

Parking Recording

DAS-7000FHD is continuously operating even vehicle is parking, and save recorded files which are created by detected any kinds of motion around of the vehicle or shocks as ‘Parking Recording’ file. These files are automatically saved into ‘MOT’ folder inside of SD memory card and it is able to call as ‘Motion recording’ in other way. Also, if DAS-7000FHD created recorded file by shocks, then those recorded files are saved as ‘Event recording’ files into ‘EVT’ folder separately. Both of Motion recording and Event recording are called by ‘Parking recording’ if those are created and saved while parking.

This Parking recording function is very useful to protect your vehicle while parking as surveillance activities with security and called by ‘Parking Surveillance Recording’.

Audio Recording

DAS-7000FHD have internal microphone and provide voice recording while above four kinds of recording mode and this voice recording function supported realism record with sound inside of the vehicle. This sound information could be valuable reference data to figure out of the accident situation sometime.

But if you do not want to use this function to protect your privacy, you can simply turn it off by pressing one button around 2 seconds where is back and bottom of the main device which is called by ‘MODE’ button. Of course, you can turn it ON back again by pressing button around 2 seconds.

Auto-reboot function for maintainable fresh operating condition

DAS-7000FHD is continuously operating while driving, even vehicle is parking with ignition is turned off for parking surveillance recording mode after installed in the first. But you able to keep and stay maintainable fresh operating condition for DAS-7000FHD with this ‘Auto-reboot function.

When you turn on the vehicle’s ignition, DAS-7000FHD is automatically rebooting so you can safely have DAS-7000FHD’s maintainable fresh operating condition without any lose recorded data.

Anti-heating system for Stable Operating in high temperature

DAS-7000FHD provide stable operating in high temperature. DAS-7000FHD’s operating temperature is -20℃ to 70℃ and storage temperature to support recorded and saved data management is -40℃ to 85℃. You able to control this configured disconnect voltage value among four kinds of levels with both 12V/24V types of voltage on OTOSAVER smartphone App or OTOSAVER PC viewer software.
